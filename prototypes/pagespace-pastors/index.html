How it works

From a passage to a Sunday-ready week

PageSpace isn't a chatbot you copy answers out of. It's a workspace where AI agents do the research, drafting, and follow-up inside your documents, using real tools — while you stay the shepherd.

1

Gather the week into one drive

Put the passage, your notes, and last year's series into pages in a single drive. Ask your agent to scope the text and pull what's relevant — it reads everything already in the drive for context, including your own study notes.

create_pageread_pagemulti_drive_search
2

The study agent does the legwork

It runs live web searches for historical and cultural background, fetches the strongest commentaries and articles as clean text, and organizes cross-references, word studies, and illustration ideas into a passage-study sheet — every claim traceable to its source.

3

The sermon drafts in the editor — you shepherd it

An outline and manuscript scaffold write straight into a Document page: big idea, movements, cross-references, and illustration slots. You write the message; the AI makes precise line edits where you ask. Refresh protection means it never clobbers the line you're working on.

create_pagereplace_lines
4

Nothing slips — care, calendar, and team

Turn prayer requests and visits into tasks with due dates, put services and counseling on the calendar, and keep your staff and worship team in sync in a channel. The week is held in one place, not in your head.
